Comeback Tour

50 Years

Fifty years on from the final episode of the Monkees TV show, the band are back together to bring their music to a new generation and their old classics to the faithful fans. With the sudden death of lead singer Davy Jones in 2012, the 3 remaining members will perform all the hits.

The Experience

The tour is described as a "full multimedia experience", displaying clips from the group's popular TV series, rare and unseen adverts and footage from the cult film, Head. The group will play their greatest hits alongside rarities and album cuts from their nearly half-century career. Making the tour unique from previous tours was the performers' choice to depart from simply doing their greatest hits, and choosing instead to include many rare songs.
